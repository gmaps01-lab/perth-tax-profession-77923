Frequently Asked Questions About Accounting Firm

How much do you charge for individual tax return preparation in Perth?

This includes a thorough review of your income, deductions, and any investment or rental properties. We’re happy to provide a more accurate quote after a quick phone consultation or review of your documents.
How long does it take to complete my tax return with Perth Tax Professionals?
Most individual tax returns are completed within 3 to 5 business days once we have all the required information. For business clients or more complex returns, it may take up to 10 business days. During peak tax season (July-October), we recommend booking early, as availability fills up quickly.
Are your accountants licensed to operate in Western Australia?
Yes, all our accountants are fully qualified and registered with the Tax Practitioners Board of Australia. Our team also maintains memberships with CPA Australia and Chartered Accountants ANZ, ensuring we adhere to the highest ethical and professional standards.
What is the process for working with your firm on BAS and GST services for my Perth business?
First, we’ll have an initial consultation to understand your business structure and reporting needs. After setting up secure access to your accounting software (such as Xero or MYOB), we’ll collect your financial data, review transactions, and prepare your BAS statements. We’ll discuss any GST concerns, lodge your BAS with the ATO, and provide advice on compliance and cash flow. Ongoing clients receive reminder notifications ahead of each lodgement due date.
Do you offer any guarantees if there’s an issue with my tax return or ATO audit?
We stand by the quality of our work. If the ATO queries your tax return due to an error on our part, we will provide representation and rectify the issue at no additional cost. While we can't guarantee an audit won't occur, we take every step to ensure your return is accurate and compliant with current Australian tax laws.
